The Problem:

The Benefits:

MEDCheck
The Problem:
Many prescription medications have serious side effects, causing "adverse effects" that are reported to the FDA. The average consumer does not have an easy mechanism to be aware of these issues.
The Solution:
Consumers should have an easy way to research prescription medications to see the side effects, reported adverse reactions, and any recalls.
The Benefits:
A solution that enables the average consumer to visit a portal to view medicinal information, their side effects and make informed decisions.

RefusalCheck
The Problem:
Consumers need a centralized mechanism to view statistical information on food and drug refusals.
The Solution:
Consumers should have a centralized dashboard to be able to view statistical information on food and drug refusals in order to identify food and drug related implications prior to consumption.
The Benefits:
Consumers will be able to search on manufacturer(s) or product(s) and obtain useful data including number of refusals, percentage of FDA sample analysis, country of origin and refusal date.

Lumify
The Problem:
Volumes of data (structured and unstructured) account for ingestion and dissemination challenges. There is a need for organization of data in order to gauge for accurate decision-making, insights and perspectives.
The Solution:
A solution that identifies disparate data types, identifies relationships and discovers patterns for trend analysis.
The Benefits:
Users can ingest structured, unstructured, and semi-structured data from any source (including audio, image and video files), and can plot relationships either in a graph format or on a geospatial map.
Mashup
The Problem:
Government stakeholders currently use manual techniques to gather, analyze and report on data center operational costs that result in significant level of effort and delays.
The Solution:
A tool that extracts operational data from all data centers and reports on specific data center performance, allows for data center cost comparisons with other data centers and generates an automated report on a periodic basis that outlines energy and operational cost savings.
The Benefits:
A unified dashboard that automates the collection, analysis, dissemination and reporting of data center operational costs and energy savings.

LiveSafe
The Problem:
The average consumer needs quantifiable metrics and recommendations to decide where to move.
The Solution:
A tool that compares two counties using socio-economic, county quality and environmental metrics and provides recommendations on where to move.
The Benefits:
A tool that provides a unified view of all pertinent socio-economic, county quality and environmental information along with recommendations on where to move. Also the ability to compare two distinct counties.

The Solution:
